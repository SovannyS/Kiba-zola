Welcome to Kiba & Zola Bully Boards

Here at Kiba & Zola Bully boards you will find an array of healthy dog treats. One of my dogs Kiba had been dealing with very severe allergic reactions since he was a puppy. With swollen eyes, blistering paws and skin infections! Being very limited for any exciting treats and food we needed a change. After finding the right medication and diet, it was decided to find single-minimal ingredient, preservative free, allergy friendly treats. All treats on your board are made from trusted local and small businesses with intention for your fur baby!
